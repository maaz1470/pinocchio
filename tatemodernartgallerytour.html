Tour Description

Plunge into the world of modern art with our fun and educational tour of the Tate Gallery!

Housed in a revamped, disused power station, this London’s top museum houses a great collection of modern and contemporary art, including works by Rothko, Matisse, Picasso Dali and many more. Visiting art gallery with children might be overwhelming, that’s why we created a customised kid-tailored tour designed to keep the younger visitors interested and engaged. Our specialized guides will plant the seed of curiosity in your children’s hearts as they discover different art movements from Neoclassicism and Impressionism to Futurism, Symbolism, Surrealism, Pop Art and more.

Skip the long lines with your fast-access tickets and start a magical journey through the history of modern art in one of the world’s most important museums. Your expert guide will introduce you to this impressive collection focusing your attention on the most important pieces. You will see the important works of Surrealism by Dalí, Ernst, Magritte and Mirò . Learn about American Abstract Expressionism marveling at the masterpieces by Pollock and Rothko . Enjoy the vibrant colours and shapes of Pop Art as you discover the famous works by Lichtenstein and Warhol .

During this visit, your guide will share fun stories and anecdotes engaging the whole family in educational games and activities designed to help children understand the art in a playful and memorable way.

To book this original and unique Tate Modern Art Gallery Tour for Children and Families, please contact us for information and reservations at info@pinocchiotours.com. You must accompany your children at all times.
