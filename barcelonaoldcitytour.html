Tour Description

Experience a family-friendly way to explore Barcelona on this 3-hour guided tour!

Get ready to discover the most interesting sites of the Old Town, such as Gothic Quarter, Las Ramblas, and Barcelona Cathedral and learn about the history and art in the most engaging and entertaining way. Our specialised guide, who knows how to keep children interested will offer a variety of fun activities such as quizzes, trivia and treasure hunts carefully prepared for this tour to stimulate kids’ creativity and encourage discussions. As a part of the tour, you will also pause to taste a delicious churro at a local shop. Make your visit to Barcelona a memorable experience and enjoy discovering the Old City with the whole family!

We will start our tour with the visit to the Gothic quarter, the oldest part of the city where centuries-old buildings have been persevered. Walking through a labyrinth of small alleyways and bustling squares you will discover the spirit of old Barcelona and visit some popular attractions, including the church of Santa Maria del Pi, Plaça Sant Felip Neri, which is the favourite square of Gaudí, the Palau de la Generalitat de Catalunya and more.

After visiting the Gothic area we will reach El Born neighbourhood, one of the most fashionable parts of the city, where the modern vibes are interlaced with the 16th century architecture. Here we will stop for the sweet break to try the popular local churros or any other snacks our guide will recommend you.

After a short walk through the Gothic centre we will reach one of the most emblematic monuments of the city, the Barcelona Cathedral. Our guide will give you a detailed account of this 14th century masterpiece and encourage the kids to learn the history and art in a playful way through fun activities and games, including trivia and treasure hunt. You will enjoy the church’s unique architecture with Gothic style decoration and glazed windows as well as an entertaining and informative commentary from your knowledgeable local guide.

At the end of our tour we will discover the trendiest area of the Old City. You will have a guided walk through la Rambla street, which has recently become a popular area for promenade, thanks for its lively atmosphere with plane trees, flower stalls, street musicians, numerous cosy cafés and souvenir shops.

To book this unique and original Barcelona Old City Tour for Children and Families, please contact us for information and reservations at info@pinocchiotours.com. You must accompany your children at all times.
