Tour Description

Discover Rome's rich historical past on our family-focused guided excursion!

There’s so much to see and discover in Rome that it can be overwhelming, especially if you are traveling with children. That’s why we created this kid-friendly tour with the focus on history, where you will have the opportunity to explore Rome’s top landmarks such as Castel Sant'Angelo, Pantheon and Piazza Navona in a relaxed and playful way. Our goal is to provide a memorable and fun experience for the whole family where each member will be engaged discovering the treasures and wonders of Rome. Our experts guides, specialized in kids activities will engage both children and parents in treasure hunts, trivia, quizzes and other interactive games organized to keep the tour entertaining and didactic at the same time.

We will start our adventure tour with Castel Sant’Angelo—one of the most iconic buildings of the city. Originally built as a tomb for the Emperor Hadrian and his family, it was transformed by Popes into their private residence, and then used as a prison, as a place for public executions and as the State Treasury. You will discover how the Castle changed over the centuries and learn many interesting historical facts, including the story about mysterious Passetto di Borgo – an elevated passageway that links the Vatican City with the Castle, used as an escape route for Popes in danger. Then, your guide will take you through the current museum housed in the fortress to show you its impressive collection of artworks, the Pope's apartments and the treasure room.

A pleasurable walk through the city centre will bring us to the next stop of our tour – the magnificent Pantheon. This ancient Roman temple survived almost intact through the years. Originally built as a pagan temple dedicated to all the gods it was then converted into a Christian Church during the medieval era. In the company of you professional tour guide you will head straight inside to explore the history, symbolism, and architectural features of the Temple. You will enjoy its unique interior crowned with the huge circular dome, one of the largest in the world since the ancient times while following the kid-friendly commentary from your guide with lots of facts, legends and curiosities about this historic treasure. Here you will also be able to see the tombs of the legendary Renaissance artist Raphael and Italian King Vittorio Emanuele II.

At the end of our tour you will discover another emblematic landmark of Rome – Piazza Navona. This historic square was built on the site of the Domitian Stadium, where in ancient times athletic competitions and games were held. Today, it is one of the most popular tourist areas in Rome with vivid atmosphere and a plenty of monuments worth visiting. Here you will discover Bernini's Four River Fountain, Borromini's church of Santa Agnese in Agone, the Egyptian Obelisk, the Pamphili Palace and much more. Your expert guide will engage both children and parents in our amazing scavenger hunts and other playful activities to make your visit to Rome a memorable and exciting experience for the whole family!
