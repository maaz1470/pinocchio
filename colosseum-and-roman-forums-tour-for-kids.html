Tour Description

Visit, learn and play around the house of the Emperors!

The Colosseum Tour is a 3-hour adventure that takes you and your family through the highlights of the ancient amphitheater and city of Rome found within what is now the ancient Roman Forum archeological park.

We will first skip past the long entrance lines to see the interior of the Colosseum, the most famous amphitheater of Roman Empire. After you learn about the history of the arena, the gladiators who fought there, and the people who were entertained you will then visit the ancient forum and walk the streets where the Romans once lived.

At the Roman Forum you will see the ruins of the Emperors' Palace, the Arch of Titus, ancient temples, and important political buildings from the time of the great Roman Empire. From the Palatine Hill you will see a spectacular panorama of the city of Rome.

We finish our tour adventure at the Capitoline Hill where you then re-enter modern Rome after an unforgettable learning experience about the ancient Romans that you won’t get in any classroom anywhere!

To book this original and unique Colosseum and Roman Forum Tour for Children and Families, please contact us for information and reservations at info@pinocchiotours.com. You must accompany your children at all times.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS ABOUT OUR COLOSSEUM AND FORUMS TOUR FOR KIDS AND FAMILIES

These are the most common questions asked by our past guests about our this tour. You few have more do not hesitate to contact us.

  • 1

    DO YOU PROVIDE TRANSFER FROM THE HOTEL OR THE AIRPORT?

    Yes, over the years we have arranged private transfers in comfortable air-conditioned minivans fully equipped with what a family needs with pick up from the hotel in and outside Rome. We also pick up from the airports or the port of Civitavecchia if you disembark with a cruise ship.

  • 2

    HOW MUCH IN ADVANCE IS PREFERABLE TO BOOK?

    The licensed guides that are good in touring with kids are not many and in high season they might not be available last minute. It is always preferable to book well in advance at least 4 weeks.

  • 3

    CAN WE PAY BY BANK TRANSFER OR CREDIT CARD?

    Yes, we accept both. If you travel from oustide the European Union credita card fees might be cheaper than banck transfer.

  • 4

    CAN THE TOUR BE CUSTOMIZED?

    Yes, most of our tours in Rome are private tours so we can customize the starting time, the lenght and the attraction visitied on your desires and needs. We will suggest you our best options and we decide together.

  • 5

    IS THE TOUR SUITABLE FOR KIDS BELOW 5 YEARS OLD?

    kids below 5 years old are welcome and they can definetely come along with a stroller as well. However, the tour is great for children aged 6 and above. Of course the guide will customize the tour based on your kids age. If they are teenagers they will have a different tour than if they are below 10 years old.

  • 6

    WHAT SHOULD WE BRING ON TOUR

    The area is mostly outdoor so it is preferable you bring comfortable shoes, a bottle of water in the hot sunny days and un umbrella in case of bad weather.

  • 7

    WILL THE TOUR TAKE PLACE WITH RAIN?

    The tour takes place with rain or shine. Much of the tour is also indoor, inside the Vatican Museums or the Basilica of Saint Peter.
