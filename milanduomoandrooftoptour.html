Tour Description

Make your visit in Milan a memorable experience for the whole family with our kid-focused tour of Duomo and its rooftop!

Milan Cathedral or simply the Duomo is a true architectural wonder and the third largest catholic church in the world. You will enjoy its white-marble façade decorated with impressive gothic style statues while following an entertaining and informative commentary from your guide, who knows how to keep alive the attention of both children and parents. Skip the long lines with your fast access tickets and head straight inside the Duomo to discover its majestic architecture, the artworks and the history in the most engaging and playful way.

After visiting the Church, you will take an elevator to the terraces and soak up a fantastic view of the city's skyline. Weather permitting, you will also be able to see The Alps and the Apennine Mountains on the horizon.

We know that happy children make happy parents, what’s why our goal is to provide a unique and personalised experience where the whole family will have fun discovering the city and its main monuments. Our top-rated guides and experts in kids’ activities will keep your children engaged and entertained making them learn and play through kid-friendly commentary and interactive activities. Both children and parents will enjoy our trivia, quizzes, treasure hunts and other games designed to encourage discussions and stimulate kids' natural creativity.

To book this original and unique Milan Duomo and Rooftop Tour for Children and Families, please contact us for information and reservations at info@pinocchiotours.com. You must accompany your children at all times.
