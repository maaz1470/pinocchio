Tour Description

Enjoy our kid-friendly tour of Pisa, one of the most beautiful Italian cities and home to world-famous landmarks!

This private tour is led by a specialized guide, who will ensure that your children are at the center of attention. Your family will discover the world of art and architecture in Pisa through interactive games, thrilling quizzes and other incredible activities. Let your kids learn about the rich past of Pisa and its UNESCO World Heritage sites in an age-appropriate way: it will be an adventure for both adults and children!

During the tour, you will stroll through the historical center of Pisa, starting from the medieval city walls, and ending in Piazza dei Cavalieri which is home to plenty of remarkable buildings whose history is deeply linked to the political and social evolution of the city. You will see the monument to Cosimo I de’ Medici, the Palazzo del Consiglio dei Dodici and the Clock Palace. Next you will reach Miracles' Square, where the famous Leaning Tower still stands after 9 centuries. Here, if you want you can climb the steps up to the top of the tower. Our local guide will provide insights and anecdotes of this beloved symbol of Italy in the world.

Finally, we will walk into the Duomo and the Baptistery, two impressive examples of Romanesque and Gothic architecture. The church, dedicated to Santa Maria Assunta, towers over the surrounding area thanks to its huge size and the beautiful decorations of the facade; the Baptistery itself is no less astonishing: it is the largest in the world!

This tour is probably the best gift you can give to your family when visiting Pisa and Tuscany original tour and you will create many lasting memories with your loved ones!

To book this original and unique Pisa Guided Tour for Kids and Families, please contact us for information and reservations at info@pinocchiotours.com. You must accompany your children at all times.
