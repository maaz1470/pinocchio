Tour Description

See the highlights of London in a family-oriented way on our private walking tour!

Make you visit to London a memorable experience for the whole family with our kid-tailored private tour. Travelling with children might be challenging, that’s we we designed a specialized itinerary for you where each member will be involved and entertained discovering the secrets and wonders of London. With our expert guide, who knows how to keep kids interested you will explore the must-see sites of the city and learn the history and art with maximum comfort and enjoyment.

Our family adventure tour will start from Westminster Abbey. You will enjoy its majestic facade with Gothic style decoration and glazed windows as you listen to informative and fun commentary about the Cathedral’s long history. You will discover where Britain's kings and queens are traditionally crowned and also where they are eventually buried.

Next, we will see the Houses of Parliament with its iconic Elizabeth Tower clock, widely known as Big Ben. Our kid-friendly guide will provide you with historical background about the British Parliament and take you then to Downing Street where the residences and official offices of Her Majesty's British Government are housed.

After walking through the bustling lively streets of the city centre, full of history, legends and unique charm we will reach the last stop of our tour, the Buckingham Palace. It is a place where the King resides when he is in London and where, on Mondays, Wednesdays, Fridays, and Sundays, can be seen the amazing spectacle of the changing of the guard.

Our family adventure tour will take you to the most interesting and beautiful sites of London. Our kid-friendly guide will keep alive the attention of your children making them learn history while having fun. Both children and parents will enjoy our fun and educational activities such as trivia and treasure hunts carefully elaborated for this tour to encourage kids’ curiosity and make your visit to London a truly special and unique one.

To book this original London Highlights Walking Tour for Children and Families, please contact us for information and reservations at info@pinocchiotours.com. You must accompany your children at all times.
