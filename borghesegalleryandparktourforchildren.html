Tour Description

An exciting journey through the treasures of the Borghese family!

Explore one of the most important museums in Rome and visit the picturesque gardens of Villa Borghese with your kids. You will discover the treasures of the residence of the noble Roman family Borghese. You will play and visit the impressive collections of the museum including some of the greatest Italian Renaissance and Baroque artists such as Titian, Raphael, Caravaggio, Rubens, Botticelli and many more. Above all you will love the world-famous marble sculptures by Bernini, especially the Apollo and Daphne.

After the gallery, you will explore the beautiful surrounding gardens that are a great combination of nature and classic art. Your guide will take you through the park and its monuments such as the neoclassical Temple of Diana and the small but pretty artificial lake of Esculapius with plenty of swans and ducks. Then you will reach the impressive Antinous obelisk and the Pincio Terraces with one of the most popular panoramas of the city.

On this kid-friendly tour you will explore the treasures of Italian art in a playful and educational manner. Your specialized guide will teach you about the artworks and history behind them in a fun and entertaining way. You will participate in various interactive activities and quizzes which will encourage both children and parents to learn and appreciate art.

To book this original and unique Borghese Gallery and Park Tour for Children and Families, please contact us for information and reservations at info@pinocchiotours.com. You must accompany your children at all times.
