Tower of London Tour for Kids and Families

We are proud to run the most famous and highly rated Tower of London tour for kids! Explore one of London iconic monuments with a special children-friendly guide exclusive for your family. On this memorable experience you will visit the castle, see the Crown Jewels, meet the Ravens, the Beefeaterss and much more. If you need a private transfer from your hotel and back we can arrange that. We take care of everything so to make your London experience memorable.

Tour Description

Explore the Tower of London – a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the city’s most emblematic attractions on our family-friendly tour!

Travelling with kids can be daunting for parents that’s why we designed this family-friendly experience where each member will be involved discovering the history and secrets of London Tower. Take a journey through the centuries and explore the Crown Jewels, the White Tower, the Green Tower, the Medieval Palace, Mint Street and many other interesting sights.

Your specialized guide will entertain you with the old legends and curiosities about the fortress: you will discover why the ravens kept at the fortress are called the "Guardians of the Tower of London", learn interesting facts about the collection of treasures from the Crown Jewels and enjoy the thrilling stories about the famous prisoners held in the Tower. Unravel the secrets of the word’s most famous Tower and learn and play with the whole family!

To book this unique and original Tower of London Tour for Children and Families, please contact us for information and reservations at info@pinocchiotours.com. You must accompany your children at all times.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS ABOUT OUR TOWER OF LONDON TOUR FOR KIDS AND FAMILIES

These are the most common questions asked by our past guests about our this tour. You few have more do not hesitate to contact us.

  • 1

    ARE YOUR GUIDES LICENSED AND PROFESSIONAL?

    All our guides are professional blue-badge licensed with many years of experience in touring with kids and families.

  • 2

    DO YOU PROVIDE TRANSFER FROM THE HOTEL OR THE AIRPORT?

    Yes, over the years we have arranged private transfers in comfortable air-conditioned minivans fully equipped with what a family needs with pick up from the hotel in and outside London or the airports.

  • 3

    HOW MUCH IN ADVANCE IS PREFERABLE TO BOOK?

    The licensed guides that are good in touring with kids are not many and in high season they might not be available last minute. It is always preferable to book well in advance at least 4 weeks.

  • 4

    CAN WE PAY BY BANK TRANSFER OR CREDIT CARD?

    Yes, we accept both. If you travel from oustide the European Union credita card fees might be cheaper than banck transfer.

  • 5

    CAN THE TOUR BE CUSTOMIZED?

    Yes, most of our tours in London are private tours so we can customize the starting time, the lenght and the attraction visited on your desires and needs. We will suggest you our best options and we decide together. If you want a shorter tour than 3 hours we can do it.

  • 6

    IS THE TOUR SUITABLE FOR KIDS BELOW 5 YEARS OLD?

    kids below 5 years old are welcome and they can definetely come along with a stroller as well. However, the tour is great for children aged 6 and above. Of course the guide will customize the tour based on your kids age. If they are teenagers they will have a different tour than if they are below 10 years old.

  • 7

    IS THE VISIT TO THE CROWN JEWELS INCLUDED IN THE TOUR?

    Yes you will see the Crown Jewels and other treasures kept in the Tower.

  • 8

    WILL WE SEE THE BEEFEATERS?

    Yes, you will meet the Beefeaters (that is anickname for the Yeomen Warders), the guardians of the Tower of London. Probably they will allow kids to take a pic with them.

  • 9

    WILL THE TOUR TAKE PLACE WITH RAIN?

    The tour takes place with rain or shine. The tour is mostly outdoor with some indoor parts.
