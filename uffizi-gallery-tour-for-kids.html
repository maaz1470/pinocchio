Tour Description

Discover famous artworks by Botticelli, Michelangelo, da Vinci, Caravaggio, Giotto, Raphael and more!

Take a fantastic journey through the Uffizi Museum with your private kid-friendly guide! During this adventure tour the whole family will be engaged in treasure hunts, trivia and other playful activities to discover the treasures of Renaissance art.

Make sure you arrive at the meeting point on time, and then you and your top rated guide will head towards the Uffizi Gallery. Thanks to your pre-paid skip-the-line tickets, you can go straight in and view the most beautiful works of art in Florence's leading museum.

The collection of the Uffizi is huge and retraces the rich history of Italian and Florentine art, from its beginnings in the eleventh century to Botticelli and the flowering of the Renaissance. In the central part of the gallery you can admire the Medici's private collection, left as a legacy to the city in the 18th century.

You will discover some of the most famous paintings of all time, including the works of the Renaissance era by Da Vinci, Michelangelo, Lippi and Raffaello, and other masterpieces such as Leonardo's ‘Annunciation’, Titian's ‘Venus of Urbino’, Botticelli's ‘Primavera’, and Caravaggio's ‘Bacchus’ and ‘Medusa’.

This entertaining tour is perfect for the whole family, every participant will be happy to discover such beauty, and kids will enjoy learning with fun trivia, filling in their worksheets and scorecards, and taking part in interactive activities including quizzes and games.

To book this original and unique Uffizi Gallery Tour for Children and Families , please contact us for information and reservations at info@pinocchiotours.com. You must accompany your children at all times.
