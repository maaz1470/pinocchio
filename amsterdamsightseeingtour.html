Tour Description

Explore the highlights of Amsterdam on our fun 2,5-hour private walking tour designed for kids & families!

Take a guided walk through lovely canals and medieval streets to discover Amsterdam’s rich historical past. You will be immersed in the vibrant atmosphere of the city with its colorful houses, cute cafes, family-friendly restaurants, cobbled streets and beautiful monuments with great artistic and historic importance. Our specialized guide will lead you in this journey engaging the whole family in tailor-made, educational activities to help you learn about the city and its culture in a playful way.

During this adventure tour you will visit the most evocative sites of the historic center and learn how Amsterdam has evolved over the centuries to become the lively European city that it is today. You will visit the Oude Kerkthe, the oldest church in the city and discover how this Catholic building was transformed in the national symbol of Dutch Protestantism. Then, we will take a moment to see the Begijnhof a medieval inner courtyard where the women of the Catholic sisterhood lived and discover how this unique institution had survived during the 16th century when the catholic religion was banned in the Netherlands. Next, we will reach the Spooksteeg, Ghost Alley in English and delve into the legend of the oldest ghost in the city, a woman from the 18th century named Helena.

After walking through the medieval area we will visit the different side of the city - Zeedijk, also known as Amsterdam's Chinatown. Here, surrounded by Asian markets, restaurants and shops, you can feel the authentic atmosphere of Oriental cultures and you will be also surprised to find the street signs in Chinese as well as Dutch. Your guide will entertain you with fun historic facts about this area and introduce you to its main attraction - the Buddhist temple.

At the end of our tour we will see the Royal Palace of Amsterdam, one of the three palaces still used by the Royal Family. You will enjoy its majestic architecture and discover the fascinating stories about Dutch kings and Queens who still receive important guests here.

We know that traveling with kids might be challenging, that’s why our goal is to provide you a family-adventure tour where each member will be involved and entertained learning the art and history of Amsterdam. Our experts guides, specialized in kids activities will engage both children and parents in treasure hunts, trivia, quizzes and other interactive games and will make you visit to Amsterdam a memorable and unique experience.

To book this unique and original Amsterdam Sightseeing Tour for Children and Families, please contact us for information and reservations at info@pinocchiotours.com. You must accompany your children at all times.
