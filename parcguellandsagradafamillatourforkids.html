Tour Description

Visit the crazy creations of Antoni Gaudì!

On this family-friendly tour you will explore both Park Guell and Sagrada Familia, two of the most incredible sites in Barcelona, both protected by UNESCO World heritage Commission.

Park Guell is a 40 acre collection of natural and artistic wonders including mosaics of animals, snakes, parrots, salamanders, eagles and other species as well as innovative architectures.

The Sagrada Familia is a fantastic church that is still not finished since 130 years of works. Designed by famous architect Antoni Gaudì, it is one of the worlds' most beautiful temples and both the facade and the interior deserve a visit.

Skip the long lines and visit both the Sagrada familia and Park Guell with this 3-hour family-friendly -tour. Play and learn by games, trivia nd activities that engage children and make your experience fun and infomative.

To book this original and unique Pak Guell and Sagrada Familla Tour for Children and Families, please contact us for information and reservations at info@pinocchiotours.com. You must accompany your children at all times.
