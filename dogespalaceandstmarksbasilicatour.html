Tour Description

Discover with fun the iconic sights of Venice on our combo tour of St. Mark’s Basilica and the Doge’s palace!

Travelling with kids can be challenging, that’s way we designed this family adventure tour where both children and parents will be actively involved learning art and history of Venice. Enjoy your skip-the-line access to the city's top attractions and take a historical journey to the times of powerful Venetian Republic.

Visit the Doge Palace and discover its amusing highlights, such as the collection of the ancient weapons and the Renaissance masterpieces by Titian, Tintoretto and Veronese. You will see the beautiful private chambers and the Doge’s apartments, the mysterious Bridge of Sights and the New Prisons, where enemies of the Republic, including Casanova were held. Kids will enjoy the thrilling stories about people who were kept in the prisons and learn with fun through the interactive activities, such as treasure hunts and quizzes carefully curated for this family-oriented tour.

Next, we will explore the main symbol of Venice, the St. Mark’s Basilica, which is considered one the most beautiful churches of Italy. Get ready to be amused by a glittering world of gold as you discover the San Marco’s majestic mosaics and other treasures within the church, including the Iconostasis and the Pala d’Oro – a 12th-century Byzantine altarpiece. Our guide will share interesting facts and curiosities about the Cathedral and explain why St. Mark became the patron of Venice.

To book this original and unique Doge's Palace and St. Mark's Basilica Tour for Children and Families , please contact us for information and reservations at info@pinocchiotours.com. You must accompany your children at all times.
