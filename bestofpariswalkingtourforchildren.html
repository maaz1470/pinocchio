Tour Description

A fun and playful experience around the main monuments and landmarks!

Paris, located on the Seine River in the north of France, has inspired artists, writers, and other creatives for centuries. Evidence shows it has been occupied by man since ancient times and today it is one of the top tourist destinations of the world. There are so many places to be seen in Paris and on our Paris City Tour you will see a selection of some of the most historic and iconic sites and also some secret spots most never know to seek out on their own.

Paris is used as the backdrop for many movies every year. Before you arrive to Paris, watch some of your favorite movies to see how many sites you can recognize on our walk. Your kids favorites may include the animated movies such as the Hunchback of Notre Dame, Ratatouille, and The Aristocats. Some of your favorites could be Devil Wears Prada, Midnight in Paris, Da Vinci Code, Inception, Rush Hour 3, and A View To Kill.

Our private, English-speaking guide will lead you on a walk along la Seine to show you a selection of sites such as the iconic Eiffel Tower, les Invalides, Place de la Concorde, the Louvre Museum, Sainte-Chapelle, la Conciergerie, Notre Dame Cathedral, and many more surprises. He or she will explain the history and significance of the sites that will make the walk both educational and more interesting for your family.

To book this original and unique Best of Paris Tour for Children and Families, please contact us for information and reservations at info@pinocchiotours.com. You must accompany your children at all times.
