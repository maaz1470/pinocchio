Tour Description

Enjoy our guided walking tour around Florence with a family-friendly focus!

Florence is a picturesque city in the heart of Tuscany and a birthplace of the Italian Renaissance. During this kid-oriented tour, the art and history will come alive as you explore the main sites of the city in the company of our expert guide who will engage both children and parents in playful and educational activities, including trivia, treasure hunt and much more!

We will take a historic journey back in time and discover how the city was founded and evolved through the centuries. Walking through the picturesque streets of Florence, full of Renaissance charm, you will learn about city’s rich history, its breath-taking architecture and famous artist who worked there and made Florence one of the most beautiful cities of Italy. Our guide will show you the highlights of the city as well as its hidden corners engaging you in the art and history on a very personal level.

During this family-adventure tour we will see the city’s most important attractions, including the picturesque squares, such as Piazza Santa Croce, Piazza del Duomo, Piazza della Signoria and explore the beautiful Renaissance churches and historic monuments that overlook them. We will walk across the famous Ponte Vecchio, surrounded by glittering jewelry shops and discover its long and curious history. Then, we will stroll around the leather market and the Mercato Nuovo, where tourists pass to rub the nose of ‘Il Porcellino’ for good luck. You will learn about the Florentine great food culture as we visit the city’s oldest food market, where we may even make a brief stop for artisan gelato or other traditional sweet.

Our specialized guides will make sure that you explore the city in the most entertaining and memorable way and ensure you a truly unique and personalized experience.

To book this original Highlights of Florence Walking Tour for Children and Families , please contact us for information and reservations at info@pinocchiotours.com. You must accompany your children at all times.
